Welcome to FemIDEAS
We work on developing survivor-centered, feminist, intersectional and abolitionist understanding and responses to Sexual and Gender Based Violence (SGBV) within Higher Education (HE). Through this project we also aim to bridge the gap between theory, policy, and practice by bringing together insights and experiences from survivors, activists, academics, and policy makers from five countries: Brazil, Chile, New Zealand, Nigeria. We are funded by UKRI and based at the University of Westminster, London.
